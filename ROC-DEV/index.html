ROC-DEV

ROC-DEV is a project me and a team of 5 people developed for 6 weeks for school.
the ROC-DEV project was to make a secure planning page for the school.
The project was made using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL.
I was responsible for the front end of the login page and some of the back end, along with multiple functions such as password reset through email.
me and my team went through alot of trouble, but we managed to get the project done, and learned alot of new things along the way.
